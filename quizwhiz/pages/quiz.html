How to Use

Questions

Select how many questions you want in your quiz. Choose from preset numbers or enter a custom amount.

Deck Selection

Choose which deck(s) to include. Select "All Decks" to mix questions from all your flashcard sets.

Difficulty

Filter questions by difficulty level. Choose "All" for a mixed challenge or focus on specific levels.

Quiz Modes

Normal: Take your time to think through each question.
Timed: Challenge yourself with time pressure for each question.

Navigation Tips

  • Enter Submit your answer
  • Esc End quiz early
  • ↑ ↓ Navigate between choices
