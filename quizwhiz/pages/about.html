What is QuizWhiz?

QuizWhiz is a comprehensive study application designed to help you master any subject through interactive flashcards, challenging quizzes, and innovative mixed study sessions. Whether you're preparing for exams, learning a new language, or expanding your knowledge, QuizWhiz adapts to your learning style.

The Inspiration Behind This Project

QuizWhiz was born out of love, frustration, and the desire to help. I created this web app for the love of my life, who has been tirelessly reviewing for her upcoming Board Exams—the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) and the Nutritionist-Dietitian Licensure Examination (NDLE). Watching her devote so much time and effort to studying, only to be met with paywalled apps and limited features, was disheartening. I wanted to make things a little easier for her.

So, instead of settling for expensive and restrictive study tools, I decided to build one myself—a tool that she could use freely, anytime, and without limitations. What began as a personal passion project soon became something more. I realized that if this app could help her, it could help many others, too.

Key Features

Smart Flashcards

Create, organize, and study with intelligent spaced repetition algorithms that adapt to your learning pace.

Dynamic Quizzes

Generate multiple-choice, true/false, and fill-in-the-blank questions automatically from your content.

Mixed Study Mode

Combine flashcards and quizzes for optimal learning with varied question types and formats.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your performance with detailed statistics and insights into your learning progress.

Flexible Import/Export

Support for multiple file formats including JSON, XML, CSV, PDF, and DOCX for easy content management.

Customizable Themes

Light, dark, and auto themes for comfortable studying in any environment or time of day.

Offline Support

Study anywhere without internet connection - all your data is stored locally on your device.

Getting Started

Ready to supercharge your learning? Here's how to get started:

  1. Create Flashcards: Add your study material using the Flashcards section
  2. Import Content: Upload existing study materials in various formats
  3. Choose Your Mode: Select from Flashcards, Quiz, or Mixed study modes
  4. Track Progress: Monitor your improvement in the Settings section
  5. Customize: Adjust themes and settings to match your preferences

Study Tips

Focus Sessions

Use the Mixed Mode with shorter durations (10-15 minutes) for focused, intensive study sessions.

Regular Review

Review difficult flashcards more frequently. Mark cards as 'hard' to see them more often.

Track Progress

Check your statistics regularly to identify areas that need more attention.

Organize Content

Use different decks to organize topics and create focused study sessions.

Technical Details

QuizWhiz is built with modern web technologies:

  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
  • Storage: Local Storage for offline functionality
  • Architecture: Modular design with separate managers for different features
  • Accessibility: WCAG compliant with keyboard navigation and screen reader support
  • Performance: Optimized for fast loading and smooth interactions
